Paid License - Paying for the rights to use a softare for set ammount of time 
Shareware - Software that is free to use and share
Trial Ware - Having a free trial for a software for a set ammount of time
Ad-ware - Software that makes its money from ads
CrippleWare - Software that is dumbed down to attract people to pay money to have the
	full version
